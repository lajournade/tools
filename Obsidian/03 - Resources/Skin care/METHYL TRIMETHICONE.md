#### Description

Methyl Trimethicone is a **very light, volatile silicone** (it evaporates from the skin rather than absorbs into it) that's similar to super commonly used [Cyclopentasiloxane](https://incidecoder.com/ingredients/cyclopentasiloxane) but it dries even faster when applied to the skin.
